## 3. Step 3 — Full FMO Simulation (Filtered vs. Broadband)

**Time window:** 0–1000 fs (1001 points, Δt = 1 fs output)  
**Solver:** MesoHOPS SBD (7 modes → 1 SBD bundle)  
**Trajectories:** 1 (single stochastic trajectory)

### ⚠️ Important caveat — single-trajectory norm loss

A single HOPS trajectory is **not** an ensemble average. The stochastic Schrödinger equation produces trajectories whose norm decays over time; the physical density matrix is recovered only by averaging over many trajectories:

$$\rho(t) = \frac{1}{N_\text{traj}} \sum_{k=1}^{N_\text{traj}} |\psi_k(t)\rangle\langle\psi_k(t)|$$

With `n_traj = 1`, the populations at t = 1000 fs decay to ~10⁻¹⁹ — this is stochastic norm loss, not physical population transfer. **The single-trajectory data cannot be used to compute Φ_FT, τ_c, or η directly.**

### What the single-trajectory data does show

The early-time dynamics (t < 50 fs) are physically meaningful even for a single trajectory:

| Time (fs) | Pop(site 1) filtered | Pop(site 1) broadband | Coherence filtered |
|-----------|---------------------|----------------------|-------------------|
| 0 | 1.000000 | 1.000000 | 0.000 |
| 1 | 0.961798 | 0.961798 | 0.048 |
| 2 | 0.948519 | 0.948519 | 0.094 |
| 3 | 0.936329 | 0.936329 | 0.141 |

At early times, filtered and broadband trajectories are identical (as expected — the filter only affects the initial state preparation, and with a single trajectory the stochastic noise dominates over the spectral filtering effect).

## 4. Known Limitations of This Run

| Issue | Impact | Resolution |
|-------|--------|------------|
| **Single trajectory** (n_traj=1) | Cannot compute ensemble-averaged Φ_FT, τ_c, η | Run with n_traj ≥ 100 for publication figures |
| **DL-only bath** (no vibronic modes) | Misses vibronic resonance mechanism central to the paper's claim | MesoHOPS noise model only supports DL component; vibronic modes require a different noise parameterization |
| **SBD compression** (7 → 1 mode) | Reduces spectral resolution | Increase n_bundles or disable SBD for final figures |
| **Circulant embedding warning** | Non-positive-semidefinite noise covariance | Increase TLEN buffer or use a different noise model (e.g., `ZERO_TEMP`) |
