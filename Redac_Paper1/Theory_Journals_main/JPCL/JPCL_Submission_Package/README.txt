KEY FINDINGS:

1. Spectral bath engineering extends excitonic coherence lifetimes by 20-50%
2. Energy transfer efficiency enhanced by 25% at room temperature (295 K)
3. Mechanism: Vibronic resonance selectively suppresses pure dephasing
4. Effect persists under physiological disorder (σ = 50 cm⁻¹)
5. Validated by 12-test suite (1.8% deviation from HEOM)
6. Three-site model demonstrates mechanism generality
