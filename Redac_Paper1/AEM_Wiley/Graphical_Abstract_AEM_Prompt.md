## Style Guidelines

### DO:
- ✅ Use clean, minimalist design
- ✅ Maintain consistent icon style throughout
- ✅ Use arrows to show flow/connection between panels
- ✅ Highlight key numbers prominently
- ✅ Ensure colorblind accessibility (avoid red-green only distinctions)
- ✅ Leave adequate white space between elements
- ✅ Use sentence case for all text labels

### DON'T:
- ❌ overcrowd with too much detail
- ❌ Use decorative fonts or excessive styling
- ❌ Include references or citations
- ❌ Use low-resolution images or pixelated graphics
- ❌ Place text too close to edges (keep 20 px margin)
- ❌ Use ALL CAPS except for acronyms (OPV, PCE, ETR, etc.)

## Technical Notes for Designer

1. **This is a computational study** - use "simulated", "predicted", or "projected" where appropriate (not "fabricated" or "measured")

2. **Transmission windows are key** - emphasize 750 nm and 820 nm as these target FMO vibronic resonances

3. **Pareto frontier is critical** - shows trade-off between PCE and ETR, with 3 configurations highlighted

4. **Dual-use concept** - always show both energy (solar panel) and agriculture (crops) together

5. **Quantum enhancement** - coherence lifetime extension (20--50%) and ETR enhancement (25%) are the key quantum metrics
